# Selective Sync in CloudSync Pro

## Overview
Choose which folders to sync to your device. This saves disk space by keeping files in the cloud only. Right-click any folder and select "Sync Settings" to configure.

## How to Use
1. Open CloudSync Pro
2. Navigate to the Selective Sync section
3. Follow the in-app instructions

## Tips and Best Practices
- Use this feature regularly to get the most value
- Explore all available options in settings
