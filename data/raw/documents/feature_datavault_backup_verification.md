# Backup Verification in DataVault

## Overview
Automatically verify backup integrity after each backup completes. Enable in Settings > Advanced > Verification to ensure your backups are reliable.

## How to Use
1. Open DataVault
2. Navigate to the Backup Verification section
3. Follow the in-app instructions

## Tips and Best Practices
- Use this feature regularly to get the most value
- Explore all available options in settings
- Check our video tutorials for detailed walkthroughs
