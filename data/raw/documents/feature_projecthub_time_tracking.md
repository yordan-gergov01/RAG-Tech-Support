# Time Tracking in ProjectHub

## Overview
Track time spent on tasks and generate reports. Start timer from any task or enter time manually. View reports in Analytics section.

## How to Use
1. Open ProjectHub
2. Navigate to the Time Tracking section
3. Follow the in-app instructions

## Tips and Best Practices
- Use this feature regularly to get the most value
- Explore all available options in settings
- Check our video tutorials for detailed walkthroughs
