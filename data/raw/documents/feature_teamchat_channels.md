# Channels in TeamChat

## Overview
Organize conversations by topic using channels. Create public channels for team-wide discussions or private channels for sensitive topics. Manage in the sidebar.

## How to Use
1. Open TeamChat
2. Navigate to the Channels section
3. Follow the in-app instructions

## Tips and Best Practices
- Use this feature regularly to get the most value
- Explore all available options in settings
- Check our video tutorials for detailed walkthroughs
