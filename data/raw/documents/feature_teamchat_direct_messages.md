# Direct Messages in TeamChat

## Overview
Send private messages to individuals or create group DMs. Click the + icon next to Direct Messages to start a new conversation.

## How to Use
1. Open TeamChat
2. Navigate to the Direct Messages section
3. Follow the in-app instructions

## Tips and Best Practices
- Use this feature regularly to get the most value
- Explore all available options in settings
- Check our video tutorials for detailed walkthroughs
