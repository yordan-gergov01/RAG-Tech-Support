# Video Conferencing in TeamChat

## Overview
Start instant video calls with up to 50 participants. Screen sharing and recording available on Pro plans. Click the video icon in any channel or DM.

## How to Use
1. Open TeamChat
2. Navigate to the Video Conferencing section
3. Follow the in-app instructions

## Tips and Best Practices
- Use this feature regularly to get the most value
- Explore all available options in settings
- Check our video tutorials for detailed walkthroughs
